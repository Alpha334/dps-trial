Vision and Mission of DPS

Vision Statement

To cultivate wisdom, balance, determination, love and faithfulness in our students. We strive to provide a nurturing and inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence character development and personal growth. Through a comprehensive and holistic approach to education.

Mission Statement

Our aim is to empower our students to become lifelong learners, critical thinkers, and compassionate individuals who positively contribute to the community and make a difference in the world.

Our Achievements

Academic Excellence

Our students consistently achieve high scores in state assessments, showcasing their dedication and hard work..

Community Engagement

Detlof Preparatory School actively participates in local initiatives, fostering a strong connection with our community.

Creative Arts Recognition

Our talented students receive awards in various art competitions, highlighting their creativity and artistic skills.

Extracurricular Activities

Regimental Band

The Regimental Band allows students to develop musical skills, perform at events, and build teamwork and discipline through engaging music experiences

Cadet Corps

The Cadet Corps instils leadership and teamwork, engaging students in drills and community service that promote responsibility and personal growth.

Cultural Troupe

The Cultural Troupe fosters creativity through dance and music, allowing students to celebrate diverse cultures in an interactive and fun environment.

Reading Club

The Reading program inspires a love for books, helping students improve comprehension and vocabulary through engaging stories and interactive group discussions.

Choerography

The Choreography program helps students express creativity through dance, developing coordination and teamwork skills in a fun and supportive environment.

Singing

The Singing program builds vocal skills and confidence, allowing students to express themselves through music while participating in fun performances and group activities.
